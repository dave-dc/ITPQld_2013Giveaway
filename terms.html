1. Standard Terms

1.1 Information on how to enter this Competition forms part of these conditions of entry. Participation in this Competition constitutes acceptance of these conditions of entry.

1.2 The promoter of this Competition is the Income Tax Professionals Queensland

ABN 90 108 946 854 of JAVDAR PTY LTD, Queensland (“ITP”).

2. Who Can Enter

2.1 Entry to the Competition is open to all Australian Residents aged 18 years and over who satisfy the entry requirements (“Contestants").

2.2 A condition of entry is that Contestants must ‘Like’ the Income Tax Professionals Facebook page and press ‘enter’ on the Facebook competition page.

2.3 Contestants must provide current and valid email address and phone number, which will be used to contact winners. The Contestants email address will be sent subsequent promotional material unless the entrant opts out by checking the box provided. The IP address of the entrant will be recorded for the purpose of recording the location of the entrant.

2.4 Upon entering, the user can choose to share with friends on Facebook.

2.5 Contestants may enter only once per email address or Facebook user account.

2.6 Directors, management, employees, officers and contractors (and their immediate families) of ITP or of the agencies or companies associated with this Competition are ineligible to enter. “Immediate families” refers to spouse, parent, guardian, natural or adopted child, and sibling (whether natural or adopted by a parent), whether or not they live in the same household as the director, manager, employee, officer or contractor.

3. How to Enter the Competition

3.1 The Competition has three separate draw periods ("the Competition Periods").

a) 12:01am (AEST) on 10/07/2013 to 11.59.59 pm (AEST) on 23/07/2013.

b) 9:15am (AEST) on 24/07/2013 to 11.59.59 pm (AEST) on 6/08/2013.

c) 9:15am (AEST) on 7/08/2013 to 11.59.59 pm (AEST) on 20/08/2013.

3.2 Entry to this Competition is free and no purchase or proof of purchase of anything is required. Entry is only available on the Facebook page of ITP Queensland.

3.3 The winning entry shall be the most creative response in answer to the question "What would you do with a personal best tax return in 2013?". Winners will be selected at 9:00am the day following the close of each Competition Period respectively. Unsuccessful entries shall not be eligible for subsequent competition periods.

3.4 If the winning entrant was referred by another entrant ("the Referrer"), by the sharing of a unique URL specific to the original entrant, the Referrer will receive the minor prize (see 4.2).

3.5 If the winning entry cannot be contacted via the email and phone numbers provided within 72hrs of the initial notification email ("the Confirmation Period"), ITP Queensland reserves the right to select a new winner. In this circumstance, the Referrer will also be redrawn.

3.6 ITP will appoint judges to draw the winner. The judges’ decision is final and none of the Contestants are entitled to argue the result, the outcome of the result or the method of the selection process.

3.7 Duplicate entries are not permitted in each draw. If ITP Queensland deems any entry to be a duplicate (based on Facebook ID, phone number or email address) the duplicate entry and all subsequent entries will be inelegible.

4. Prizes

4.1 The major prizes on offer are three(3) cash prizes of AU$500.

4.2 The minor prizes on offer are three(3) retail vouchers to the value of AU$100 (including GST).

4.3 The winner of the minor prize will only be deemed eligible if the major prize winner is also eligible, and successfully confirmed within the Confirmation Period. Minor prize winners will only be notified once the Major prize winner has been confirmed.

4.4 All Prize draws will be conducted at ITP Queensland, 4/3442 Pacific Hwy, Springwood QLD 4127 or such other place or places as ITP may determine in its absolute unfettered discretion.

4.5 All winners will be notified by email. Their first name will also appear on the ITP Queensland Facebook Wall (http://www.facebook.com/itpqld).
